## Age & Weight Requirements
- Age limit: 15-69 years (under 18 requires guardian approval)

## Training Method: Tow Launch (Hinaus)
- Safe, effective way to learn paragliding basics from flat ground
- Student attached to tow rope (e.g., via ATV)
- Multiple repetitions in same conditions optimal for learning
- Years of practical experience with this method
